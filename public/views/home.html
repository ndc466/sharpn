Our Vision

“Sharpn Ministries is a biblical based ministry focused on the maintenance and enhancement of sobriety through spiritual fitness, we exist as a non-profit organization providing tools and resources for intentional recovery. As a ministry, we create environments where recovering drug addicts and alcoholics find strength and hope in their recovery and confidence in their walk with Christ. Sharpn’s classes, personal mentoring, physical fitness programs, and sober living revolve around our value of development and through systems of evaluation and goal focused adjustment we believe Sharpn offers a platform for change and the opportunity to transition from destruction to construction.”
